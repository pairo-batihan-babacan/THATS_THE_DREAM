How to Rotate PDF Pages

  1. Upload your PDF using the drop zone above.
  2. Click individual pages to select them, use Shift+Click for a range, or Ctrl+Click to toggle individual pages.
  3. Drag or right-click drag anywhere in the page grid to lasso-select multiple pages, then use the action bar to rotate the selection.
  4. Use All CW / All CCW / All 180° to rotate every page at once.
  5. Click Orientations to auto-detect portrait and landscape pages — then select by orientation or fix them all in one click with Make All Portrait or Make All Landscape.
  6. Click Apply Rotations to process and download your corrected PDF.

Rotate All Pages vs. Individual Pages

Use All CW / All CCW / All 180° when every page was scanned in the wrong orientation. Use individual or multi-page selection when only specific pages need fixing, such as a single landscape diagram within an otherwise portrait document.

Auto-detect Portrait & Landscape Pages

The Orientations button analyses every page's dimensions and classifies them as portrait (vertical, taller than wide) or landscape (horizontal, wider than tall). Each page shows a small P or L badge. You can then select all pages of one type with Select Landscape or Select Portrait, or correct an entire messed-up document in one click with Make All Portrait or Make All Landscape.

Privacy

Your PDF is uploaded over HTTPS and processed on our secure servers. The original file and the rotated result are automatically and permanently deleted within 30 minutes. We do not read, store, or share the content of your files. No account is required.
