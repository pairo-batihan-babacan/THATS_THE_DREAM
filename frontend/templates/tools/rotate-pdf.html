How to Rotate PDF Pages

  1. Upload your PDF using the drop zone above.
  2. Click individual pages to select them, or use All CW / All CCW to rotate every page at once.
  3. Drag across the page grid to select multiple pages, then use the action bar to rotate the selection.
  4. Use Auto-detect to automatically find sideways landscape pages and fix them in one click.
  5. Click Apply Rotations to process and download your corrected PDF.

Rotate All Pages vs. Individual Pages

Use All CW / All CCW when every page was scanned in the wrong orientation — for example, an entire document photographed sideways. Use individual or multi-page selection when only specific pages need fixing, such as a single landscape diagram within an otherwise portrait document.

What Is Auto-detect?

Auto-detect analyses each page's dimensions and flags pages that are wider than they are tall (landscape orientation). These are often pages scanned on their side. When detected, PDFWorks highlights them in the grid and offers to rotate them 90° clockwise in a single click.

Privacy

Your PDF is uploaded over HTTPS and processed on our secure servers. The original file and the rotated result are automatically and permanently deleted within 30 minutes. We do not read, store, or share the content of your files. No account is required.
