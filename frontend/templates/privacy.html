Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2024. This policy explains what data PDFWorks collects, how we use it, and the rights you have under GDPR and other applicable laws.

1. Who We Are

PDFWorks ("we", "us", "our") is the operator of the website at https://pdfworks.io. We provide free online file conversion tools. For privacy-related enquiries, you can contact us at privacy@pdfworks.io.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Files You Upload

When you use a PDFWorks tool, the file you upload is transmitted securely to our servers for processing. We do not read, analyse, or retain the content of your files beyond what is required to perform the conversion. All uploaded files and their output are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 30 minutes of upload.

2.2 Technical Logs

Like virtually all web services, our servers automatically record basic technical information in access logs, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymised to the first three octets for retention)
  • The date and time of your request
  • The URL you requested
  • Your browser's User-Agent string
  • HTTP status codes

These logs are used solely for server security, abuse prevention, and diagnosing technical issues. They are retained for a maximum of 14 days and are not shared with third parties except as required by law.

2.3 Cookies and Local Storage

PDFWorks uses browser local storage (not cookies) to remember your preferred colour theme (dark or light mode). This data is stored entirely in your own browser and is not sent to our servers. We do not set any tracking or advertising cookies ourselves.

2.4 Advertising

We display advertisements provided by Google AdSense to help cover the operating costs of the service. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by using the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page. You can also install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data described above only for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Processing your file conversion requests.
  • Security: Preventing abuse, fraud, and attacks on our infrastructure.
  • Performance: Monitoring and improving the reliability and speed of our service.
  • Legal compliance: Complying with applicable laws and regulations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not build profiles of individual users.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal bases for processing personal data are:

  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): We process technical log data to maintain the security and proper functioning of our service.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR): For any advertising personalisation by Google, your consent is obtained through the Google Consent Framework if applicable in your region.

5. Data Retention

  • Uploaded files: Automatically deleted within 30 minutes of upload. This cannot be extended or recovered — it is a core privacy guarantee.
  • Server access logs: Retained for a maximum of 14 days, then deleted.
  • Theme preference (local storage): Stored in your browser only. We never receive this data.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and equivalent laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): You can request deletion of your personal data. Note that uploaded files are already deleted automatically within 30 minutes.
  • Right to rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact privacy@pdfworks.io. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

7. International Transfers

Our servers are hosted in the European Union. If you access PDFWorks from outside the EU, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in the EU. Such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards.

8. Children's Privacy

PDFWorks is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the most recent update at the top of this page. Continued use of PDFWorks after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@pdfworks.io or visit our Contact page.
