Contact Us

Have a question, spotted a bug, or want to suggest a new feature? We'd love to hear from you. Choose the most relevant contact option below.

📧 General Support

For questions about how to use PDFWorks, conversion issues, or anything related to the tools, email us at:

support@pdfworks.io

We aim to respond within 2 business days. Please include details of the file type, size, and tool you were using.

🐛 Bug Reports

If you've found a bug or a conversion that produces unexpected results, please send details to:

bugs@pdfworks.io

Include the tool name, your browser and OS version, and what you expected to happen vs. what actually happened.

💼 Business & Press

For partnership enquiries, advertising questions, or press / media requests, please contact:

hello@pdfworks.io

We are open to discussions about API access, white-label arrangements, and editorial collaborations.

🔒 Privacy & GDPR

For questions about our data handling practices, to make a Subject Access Request, or to exercise your GDPR rights, contact our privacy team:

privacy@pdfworks.io

See our Privacy Policy for full details of how we handle data.

Before You Write

Many common questions are already answered in our FAQ page. Check there first — it covers topics like file size limits, supported formats, how long files are stored, and how the Pro token works.

Response Times

We are a small, focused team. We read every email and aim to reply to all genuine support requests within 2 business days (Monday–Friday, excluding public holidays). Complex technical issues may take a little longer, but we will always acknowledge receipt promptly.

What We Cannot Help With

Please note that we are unable to:

  • Recover files that have been deleted from our servers (files are removed within 30 minutes of upload — this is by design and cannot be undone).
  • Unlock or decrypt PDF files that belong to third parties — we can only help you unlock files you own.
  • Provide legal advice about copyright or data protection.
